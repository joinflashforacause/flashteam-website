Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

✅ This website is purely informational. We do not collect, store or process any personal data through this website. All our services and contributions are handled offline only.

1. About FLASH Team

FLASH (Forum for Legal, Ambulance, Social & Health Services) is a registered charitable society (Reg: 234/2004) based in Eluru, Andhra Pradesh. This website — flashteam.co.in — exists solely to share information about our work, programs, and impact with the public.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

This website does not collect any personal information from visitors. Specifically, we do not:

  • Ask for or store your name, phone number, email address or any other personal details
  • Process any online payments or contributions
  • Use contact forms, sign-up forms or registration pages
  • Add visitors to any WhatsApp groups or broadcast lists
  • Use tracking cookies, advertising cookies or behavioural analytics
  • Share any visitor data with third parties

3. Contributions & Membership

All contributions, memberships and enrollments for FLASH Team programs are handled entirely offline — in person, via phone, or through direct UPI/bank transfer communicated directly by our team. No financial transaction takes place through this website.

If you wish to contribute, please contact us directly using the details in the Contact section below.

4. Cookies & Analytics

This website does not use cookies of any kind — not for analytics, not for advertising, and not for session tracking. No third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics) are active on this site.

5. External Links

This website may contain links to external websites (for example, social media pages). Once you leave our website, we are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies separately.

6. Children's Privacy

Since this website collects no personal data from any visitor, it is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

If our website ever changes to include features that collect personal data, we will update this Privacy Policy and clearly indicate the change. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any revision.
