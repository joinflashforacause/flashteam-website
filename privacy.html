Privacy Policy

FLASH (Forum for Legal, Ambulance, Social & Health Services) is a registered charitable society (Reg: 234/2004) based in Eluru, Andhra Pradesh. This policy explains how we handle information across our website and our WhatsApp-based donor communication system.

Last updated: July 2026

1. This website

This website is purely informational. We do not collect personal data through this website itself — we do not ask visitors for names or emails, we do not process payments online, and we do not use tracking or advertising cookies here.

2. Contributions and memberships

Contributions, memberships, and enrollments for FLASH Team programs are handled offline, in person or by phone, and separately through our WhatsApp donor communication system described in Section 4 below.

3. Cookies

This website does not use cookies of any kind — not for analytics, not for advertising, and not for session tracking.

4. WhatsApp communications and donor records

Separately from this website, FLASH uses the WhatsApp Business Platform (operated by Meta) to communicate with donors and with people we have provided free ambulance or mortuary assistance to. Through this channel, we collect and store:

  • Your name and phone number
  • Where applicable, the amount and date of a contribution you have made to FLASH
  • A photo you have shared with us, used only for personalized thank-you messages

This information is stored securely using third-party infrastructure, including Render and Google Firebase, and is accessible only to authorized FLASH team members. We use it to send you messages about FLASH's services, fundraising campaigns, and events (such as Smaranotsavam), to thank you for past contributions, and to respond to messages you send us. We do not sell your data or share it with third parties other than the service providers named above, who process it solely to help us deliver these communications.

You may stop receiving messages from FLASH at any time by replying "STOP" on WhatsApp, or by contacting us using the details in Section 8 to request that we delete your data from our records.

5. External links

Once you leave our website, we are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites.

6. Children's privacy

This website itself collects no personal data from visitors and is safe for users of all ages. Our WhatsApp communications described in Section 4 are directed at adult donors and contributors, not children.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.
